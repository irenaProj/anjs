AngularJS Playground

I love working with AngularJS, it really brings traditional back-end functionality, such as dependency injection and two way data binding to the front-end. After working with J7EE technologies, such as Servlets, I can really appreciate the easiness and effectiveness of AngularJS.

AngularJS section contains Learning Material page, which is more of a "memo to self" reference and an Exercises page, currently contains a Coffee Shop - simple example project.

In addition to this small project, other AngularJS projects on this site are:

  • Q&A section - collection of Q&A on different subjects, Q&A CRUD functionality and Q&A quiz functionality, also with CRUD operations.
  • Calendar section, currently under construction. Once completed, Calendar will allow building and managing daily appointments and todo lists.
